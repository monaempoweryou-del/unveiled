Questions, answered

Do I have to pay just to see my website?+
No. You already saw your real website, free. You only choose a plan when you want to put it to work.
What's the difference between the four options?+
Three are monthly, where we run your website for you: Website Launch keeps your site live and supported, Visibility adds monthly SEO, blog posting, and Google Business Profile optimization to get you found, and Growth Machine adds the full automation: AI assistant, SMS & booking, automated follow-up, and CRM that actively bring in and follow up with customers. Own It Forever is a one-time purchase: you buy the website outright and we transfer everything to you, with no subscription — and you can still bolt on any of those services as add-ons.
Why are three plans monthly and one is one-time?+
The monthly plans are an ongoing relationship: we host, maintain, and (on Visibility and Growth Machine) actively grow your business every month. Own It Forever is a purchase, not a relationship: you own the asset and run it yourself, with optional services available whenever you want them.
I bought my site outright. Can I still add services?+
Yes. After you own your website, you can add any of three month-to-month services, priced for owners since the build is already yours: Care Plan ($97/mo) for hosting, maintenance, and support; Visibility ($397/mo) for SEO, blog posting, and Google Business Profile; and Growth Machine ($897/mo) for the full AI automation, CRM, and follow-up. Pick any, all, or none, and cancel anytime.
What happens after I click Activate?+
You confirm your plan and complete a secure checkout, then we connect your domain and take your site live, usually within 24 to 48 hours. For Own It Forever, you complete the one-time checkout (plus any add-ons you chose) and we transfer ownership of your website, hosting, and domain to you.
Can I change later?+
Yes. Start on Website Launch and add the machine whenever you're ready. Nothing rebuilds, your site and data come with you.
Is there a contract?+
No. The monthly plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime.
