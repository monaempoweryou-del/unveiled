Our Story

Right here on the Avenue.

Salon C started with a simple idea: why should Southern Humboldt have to drive 60 miles for a full-service salon? Hair, skin, tanning. All in one place, right on the Avenue of the Giants in Miranda.

We're small by design. When you walk in, we know your name. And by your second visit, we'll know your order. That's the beauty of being the neighborhood salon in a place where neighbors still matter.

Whether you're a local who's been coming since day one or a traveler stopping through the redwoods, there's a chair with your name on it.
