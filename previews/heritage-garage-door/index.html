Good to know

Questions, answered.

Can you come out the same day?

In most cases, yes. We prioritize fast response across Murrieta, Temecula and the surrounding area, and carry common parts on the truck so many repairs are completed in a single visit.

How much does a new garage door cost?

Most homeowners budget roughly $1,500–$4,500 depending on size, material, insulation and style. We'll walk you through the options and give transparent pricing before any work begins.

How long does a garage door last?

A well-installed door typically lasts 15–30 years. Springs and moving parts wear sooner, which is why timely repairs and the occasional tune-up keep everything running safely.

Should I replace a spring myself?

We don't recommend it. Springs are under high tension and can be dangerous without the right tools and training — our techs replace them safely and balance the door correctly.
