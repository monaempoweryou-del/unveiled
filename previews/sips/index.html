Our Story

A little warmth in the heart of the redwoods.

Sips started the way the best things in a small town do, out of love for good coffee and the people who'd come to drink it. We're a family-warm little window on the Avenue of the Giants, serving Southern Humboldt locals and the travelers passing through the giant trees.

Organic from the start, made fresh every day, and run by folks who'll know your order by your second visit. For the locals, this is our place. For the road-trippers, it's the friendly Humboldt you were hoping to find.
