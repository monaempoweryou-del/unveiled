About Us

Your Neighbors, Building Humboldt's Future

Time To Shine Builders is a full-service contractor born and based right here in Southern Humboldt. From a complete ground-up build to a quick repair, owner Daniel Camacho and our crew bring the skills, the equipment, and the work ethic to get it done.

We've built a huge portfolio doing a little bit of everything — framing and foundations, plumbing and septic, remodels, greenhouses, fire prep and more. If a job needs doing, chances are we've done it before.

Our whole reason for being here is simple: help our community stay local, deliver top-quality work, and keep it fair.
