Good to know

Questions, answered.

How fast can you get to me?

We run several mobile crews across the High Desert, so response is typically 15–20 minutes within Victorville, Hesperia, Apple Valley and nearby areas — 24/7, including holidays.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Mike's Locks is a licensed (California BSIS), bonded and insured locksmith company. Your home, car and business are in qualified hands.

How much does it cost?

We work on a flat-rate basis so there are no surprises. Most common services are more affordable than people expect — call and we'll give you a straight answer before any work begins.

Do you make car keys and program remotes?

Yes — we cut and program keys, transponders and push-to-start remotes for nearly every make. A new car key typically takes 40–90 minutes. Call ahead so we confirm we carry yours.

Is there a warranty?

We back our work with a 90-day guarantee on hardware and labor, and a 3-month warranty on remote keys (normal use).
