What We Do

Full-Service Doors & Windows

Whether you are replacing a single entry door or outfitting a new build with custom windows throughout, our team handles the full scope with care and expertise.

Window Replacement

Upgrade aging or drafty windows with energy-efficient, beautifully crafted replacements. We carry single-hung, double-hung, casement, sliding, and custom configurations to fit your home perfectly.

Door Installation

Entry doors, patio doors, French doors, sliding glass doors. We install and fit every door to tight tolerances so it seals cleanly, operates smoothly, and looks exactly right for years to come.

Patio & Sliding Doors

Connect your indoor and outdoor living spaces with premium sliding or multi-panel patio door systems. Smooth glide hardware, weathertight seals, and clean sight lines from day one.

Repair & Weatherization

Sticking doors, broken seals, drafty frames. We diagnose and fix the problem right the first time. Proper weatherization saves energy and keeps your home comfortable through every season.

Custom & Specialty Orders

Unique openings call for custom solutions. We work with leading manufacturers to source specialty sizes, shapes, glass types, and hardware finishes to match your design vision exactly.

Commercial Projects

From storefronts to multi-unit residential buildings, South Bay Door & Window has the capacity and experience to handle commercial-scale projects on schedule and on budget.
