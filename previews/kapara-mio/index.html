Our Story

Two worlds on one plate

Kapara Mio was born from family recipes passed down through generations — the same falafel, hummus and fluffy pita our grandparents made, now served steps from the Pacific.

We blend fine spices brought from Israel with the rich flavors of Oaxaca, and build every plate from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. It's where the desert meets the sea — honest food, made fresh, every single day.
